What is UIPMR.com portal?
UIPMR is an online registration portal for booking Appointments with all kind of Doctors around the world.
Is booking appointment compulsory to consult the doctor?
Yes.You need to book an appointment to consult the doctor.You can do that online by visiting the website.
What are the various methods to book the appointment?
Patient can get the online appointment (over internet) by visiting website uipmr.com.
What are the advantages of booking online appointment using uipmr.com?
Patients can avoid standing in long queues at the hospital for booking appointment.
What are the pre-requisites for booking appointment?
New patient has to first register himself on the website. All the required details have to be filled by patient himself.
What is the process to book appointment for a new patient?
Patients, who are already registered on UIPMR can book appointment by logging in to their account and navigating to the appointment booking section.
To book Appointment patient has to provide User name and Email Id and once the One Time Password (OTP) sent to the patient by email has been verified, he can successfully book the appointment with the concerned doctor. The appointment details will be sent to the patient through email.
